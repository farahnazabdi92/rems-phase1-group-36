# Team Roles and Responsibilities

This project is carried out as a collaborative group effort. To ensure effective coordination and accountability, specific roles have been assigned to each team member. While responsibilities are defined, all members contribute actively across all stages of the project.

## Roles

### Project Coordinator
Responsible for overall project organisation, scheduling meetings, and ensuring that deadlines are met. Acts as the main point of communication within the team.

### Requirements Lead
Leads the requirements engineering activities, including stakeholder identification, elicitation, and documentation of functional and non-functional requirements.

### Documentation Lead
Ensures consistency, clarity, and quality of all written deliverables. Responsible for compiling and formatting the final report.

### Technical Support / Analyst
Supports modelling activities, maintains the repository structure, and assists with organising diagrams, traceability, and validation artefacts.

## Collaboration Approach

All team members:
- Participate in discussions and decision-making
- Review each other’s work
- Contribute to writing and refinement of deliverables

This collaborative approach ensures shared understanding and improves the overall quality of the project.
