# REMS Project – Stakeholders Analysis

## 1. Stakeholders

### 1. Researcher

**Description:**  
Researchers are the primary users of the system. They submit ethics applications, upload required documents, track application progress, and respond to feedback from the ethics committee.

**Value / Interest:**  
- Ability to track application progress  
- Clear visibility of required documents  
- Efficient and transparent submission process  

---

### 2. Administrative Staff

**Description:**  
Administrative staff are responsible for performing initial checks on submitted applications, ensuring completeness, and managing the workflow of applications within the system.

**Value / Interest:**  
- Easily verify completeness of submissions  
- Identify missing information quickly  
- Efficient management of application workflow  

---

### 3. Ethics Committee Member

**Description:**  
Ethics committee members review assigned applications, evaluate research risks and methodology, request clarifications, and make decisions regarding approval.

**Value / Interest:**  
- View applications assigned to them  
- Communicate with researchers for clarification  
- Efficient and structured review process  

---
