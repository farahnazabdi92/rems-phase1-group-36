**Researchers** complete application forms and submit them as documents.
- Submit applications quickly through a unified system
- Receive structured feedback and request for clarification
- Manage team members' roles and responsibilities

---

**Administrative staffs** track and manage the workflow of applications.
- Track applications and ensure all steps are completed
- Access audit trails to track history and logs
- Communicate with researchers about errors or missing information

---

**Committee members** review applications and make approval decisions.
- Access all versions of documents and team members' details
- Declare conflicts of interest and recuse themselves if necessary
- Discuss review process of applications with other members
