# Interview Questions  
**Target Stakeholder: Administrative Staff**

## Stakeholder Selection Justification

Administrative staff were selected as the target stakeholder because they are involved in multiple stages of the ethics application process, including checking completeness, tracking progress, and coordinating communication between researchers and reviewers.

Unlike other stakeholders, they have visibility across the entire workflow. This makes them particularly valuable for identifying inefficiencies, gaps, and coordination issues in the current system.



### Question 1

- **Justification:**  
The scenario describes a fragmented process, but does not clearly define the exact workflow.

- **Impact:**  
This helps us understand the end-to-end process, identify key stages, and define system workflows and use cases.
