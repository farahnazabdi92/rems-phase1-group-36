Researchers: 
Role is to conduct research activities that require ethics approval on participants. Will be able to utilise a more efficient, streamlined, transparent application process.

Academic staff: 
Monitor the status of applications, inform researchers of the outcome, perform administrative checks on applications and assignment of application to committee members. System will allow for academic staff to track status of application as well as manage applications easier

Members of university Ethics Committee: 
Periodically reconstituted members whose job is to undergo ethics review of submitted research applications through discussion with other members and either approve, conditionally approve or reject. System will allow for members to avoid conflict of interests issues and also view changes over time for supporting documents.

Study participants: 
Participate in research activities. Interested in having their welfare and data protected during the application process
